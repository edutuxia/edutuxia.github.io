Meet


eduInUK is a London based educational consulting company specialising in helping Indian students’ study in the UK. Our team comprises of educational experts, many of whom were international students themselves, who understand your frustrations and worries and work around the clock to ensure that your journey is smooth and successful.

From helping you apply to the right course at the right university, guiding you through your visa application all the way to arranging your cab to your handpicked accommodation; eduInUK is with you every step of the way, just a call away.

1.3 Billion Minds, 1.3 Billion Hearts. One size does not fit all. That is why at eduInUK

  •       We provide personalized support to you from start to finish.
  •       We only focus on Indian Students - we understand your needs, ambitions and challenges.
  •       We only focus on UK universities - we are based in the UK and are alumni of the UK.
  •       We offer all our services completely free of cost.
