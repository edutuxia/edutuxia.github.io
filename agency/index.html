Your Path to the UK


1

Explore Your Options

Book your free consultation with our advisors and tell us your story, your passion, aspirations, goals and interests. We will then curate a shortlist of universities and courses that are personalized to your requirements and circumstances.

2

Design Your Career

Your personal advisor will then sit down with you (safely over zoom) and finalize your target universities. It’s time to start working towards the ultimate career you want to build.

3

Apply To Universities

From English language tests to Statements of Purpose, we will help you on every step of the way. Our dedicated application team will make and track the applications on your behalf so you can sit back and relax. At this stage, if you need Financial Assistance, our specialist advisors can help you find bank and other institutional loans.

4

Secure Your Visa

This is a crucial step in your journey and one that must be handled with utmost care. We will guide you through the entire process of applying for your visa, including checking your application form, preparing/checking supporting documents, obtaining the Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) and preparing you for the visa interview.

5

Prepare To Fly

Nearly there, but not quite yet! From finding suitable accommodation to opening a UK bank account, to figuring out how to transfer money and booking flights and cabs, let eduInUK’s partners do the hard work for you. We have special partnerships with various service providers, making eduInUK your true one-stop-shop. All free of charge, obviously.
Oh, and before you depart, enjoy our pre-departure crate of essentials (alongside a briefing) to make sure you are all set up and roaring to go.

6

Arrival in the UK

Some 8 hours of flying later, you have arrived in the UK. This country is about to transform your life. As an eduINUK student, you also automatically become a member of the National Indian Students and Alumni Union UK (NISAU). NISAU is the oldest and largest body representing Indian students and alumni in the UK and are there to look after your welfare and to help you with any problems you may have. They also host amazing events and activities throughout the year which you will be able to participate in.
